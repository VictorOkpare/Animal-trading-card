Flamingo - Phoenicopterus roseus

Habitat
Flamingos prefer shallow lakes, lagoons, and coastal waters in warm climates, particularly in Africa, Asia, and Europe. They often live in large colonies with thousands of other flamingos.
Diet
These filter feeders use their specialized beaks to strain tiny shrimp, algae, and plankton from the water. Interestingly, their pink feathers come from pigments obtained in their food!
Did you know?
Greater Flamingos are social birds known for their synchronized feeding and breeding behaviors. Their unique pink feathers and graceful movements have made them a symbol of beauty in many cultures.!
